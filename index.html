Welcome to COSMODISCOS - Your Gateway to Sonic Exploration

Dive deep into the heart of Biel's vibrant music culture with COSMODISCOS. We are not just another record shop; we are an authentic testament to the rich tapestry of music, art, and creativity that pulses through our town and beyond.

From local artists who have left their mark in Biel to the bustling sounds of globally-infused modern artists, our curated selection reverb as an echo of our expansive musical tastes and still speaks of an equally intricate connection to our roots.

Our dual turntables await your curiosity. Close your eyes and lose yourself in unfamiliar comforting rhythms, beats, and melodies. At COSMODISCOS, we're not just selling records, we're crafting memories, one note at a time. Slide in and let that record spinning!
